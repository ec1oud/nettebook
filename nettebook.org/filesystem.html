I'm serious, I suspect filesystems may be obsolete some day. They may not be replaced by the relational database but maybe an object database. Files are cumbersome; they require you to identify them by name and location rather than other kinds of associations which might come more naturally to you. In this case, the user doesn't care what the filename of the business card is, only what contact it's associated with. But filesystems may not be obsolete until they are replaced with OODBs/knowledgebases.
